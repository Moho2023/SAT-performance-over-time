About our Project

Our project will focus on demonstrating the relationship between availability of broadband access in the united states and the average educational proficiency of students living in those areas, using their mean SAT and ACT scores

Some terms to know:

  • Broadband: High speed and high bandwidth internet service
  • SAT: The Scholastic Aptitude Test, which is scored out of 1600, measures students' proficiency in high school level mathematics and reading/writing
  • ACT: Scored out of 36, the ACT measures the same thing as the SAT, and also includes an addiitonal section on science comprehension
