About our Project

Our project will focus on demonstrating the distributio of SAT scores by state across the United Sates

Some terms to know:

  • SAT: The Scholastic Aptitude Test, which is scored out of 1600, measures students' proficiency in high school level mathematics and reading/writing.

    It is most often used in. the college admissions procss.

  • SAT Math Score: The score for the math section of the SAT.
  • SAT Reading Score: The score for the reading section of the SAT.
