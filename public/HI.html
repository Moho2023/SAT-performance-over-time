Average SAT Scores and Percentages of SAT Test Takers by State

Hawaii

Some quick facts:

  • The most recent average composite score for the state of Hawaii was in 2020 and it was a 1095
  • This composite score was composed of 549 in the EBRW section and 546 in the math section
  • The national average in 2020 was 1051
  • The composite average score in Hawaii is at or higher than the national average
  • The percentage of high school students in Hawaii taking the SAT in 2020 was 51% while the national participation was 60%
